Hi! I'm Jonathan Manas aka. Athan, a passionate Front-end Developer based in the Philippines.

Coming from a Psychology background, I taught myself how to code websites. I created my first website in 2000 and have pursued professional web design and development since 2005.

I attributed Psychology, cognitive creativity, and my interest in logic and human behavior to my success and standout advantage as a Front-end Developer.

Understanding the range of the Front-end spectrum, I tend to focus on CSS, UX, web performance, and Accessibility.

Being an advocate of clean, maintainable code, my go-to CSS methodologies are BEM, OOCSS, SMACSS, and Atomic Design.
